Mission

We seek to provide a bioinformatics tool to facilitate the use, experimentation and implementation of microalgae in all fields. Them being academia, industry, arts, personal projects, among others.

Vision

CO2 Snatchers seeks for its tool to become a renowned resource, catalyst for the extensive use of microalgae, especially for CO2 capture and mitigation of greenhouse gas emissions.
